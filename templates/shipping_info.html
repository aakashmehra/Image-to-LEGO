Shipping Information

At AP Studios, we are committed to providing a seamless and efficient shipping experience for all our customers.

Shipping Locations

We currently offer shipping services to customers located in the United States and Canada.

Shipping Method & Costs

We provide free standard shipping for all orders placed through our website. AP Studios covers all standard shipping costs—there are no additional charges or fees.

Delivery Timeframe

Once an order is placed, we aim to ship it within 1-3 business days. Please note that this timeframe excludes weekends and public holidays.

Products are typically received within 14 days and are available immediately upon order placement.

Order Processing Time

We typically require 1-2 business days to process orders before they are ready for shipment.

During peak seasons or promotional periods, processing times may be slightly longer, but we always strive to ship your order as quickly as possible.

Order Tracking

We provide order tracking information for all shipments. Once your order has been shipped, you will receive an email notification with tracking details.

You can use this information to track your shipment's progress on our website or through the designated shipping carrier's website.

Please note that AP Studios is not responsible for any lost or damaged shipments caused by the shipping carrier.

Shipping Restrictions
