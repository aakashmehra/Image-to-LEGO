Building Worlds Since 2014

Discover the story behind AP Studios and our passion for creating compelling LEGO stories that inspire builders and dreamers worldwide.

AP Studios was born from a lifelong passion for LEGO and storytelling. Since 2014, we have been dedicated to producing high-quality content that inspires creativity and brings intricate brick-built worlds to life for our community of over 10,000 subscribers.

Our Journey Through Time

2014 - The Foundation

The first brick was laid. Driven by a love for stop-motion and LEGO, AP Studios was launched with a simple mission: to share unique stories with the world, one frame at a time.

2018 - Growth & Milestones

Our community grew beyond imagination. We celebrated hitting 5,000 subscribers, upgraded our equipment, and took on our most ambitious animated project to date, pushing the boundaries of our creativity.

Today - The Vision

With over 10,000 subscribers, we're focused on telling bigger, bolder stories. Our vision is to continue inspiring the next generation of builders and filmmakers, expanding our universe with new series and collaborations.

Our Philosophy

What makes AP Studios unique is our unwavering commitment to quality storytelling. We believe every brick has a story to tell. We blend meticulous animation techniques with engaging narratives and a deep respect for our community to create content that is not just watched, but felt.
